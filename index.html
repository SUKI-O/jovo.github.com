philosophy

It seems that this world is already an incredibly beautiful place to live. Perhaps we could contribute to further increasing the love felt by all beings on her. Our thesis is that by improving our models of how we think and act, we will be able to make fruitful contributions to this endeavor. More specifically, we aim to contribute by developing and applying tools from statistical decision theory to neuroscience, psychology, and beyond. Our primary foci are statistical graph theory and brain-graphs (connectomes).

Perhaps partially because we fervently believe that we are all in this together, we are strong advocates of open-science, that is, making one's research freely available to all. To that end, as a research project materializes into a state worthy of witness, we open the project's repository to all, containing a complete version history of every file, including data, figures, code, etc. The intention of this website is to share our dreams, our work, and invite you to come join our party, and perhaps inspire others to behave in accordance with their dreams.
