research overview

i am primarily interested in contributing to the development, improvement, and application of statistical models and inference techniques that characterize how brain-graphs store and process information, and otherwise contribute to mental properties. i hope these tools facilitate addressing neuroscience questions that remain unresolved or unconceived. we are motivated by the dazzling complexity of brains, and therefore try to be relatively agnostic with regard to spatiotemporal resolution and the evolutionary hierarchy. that said, our work is very application oriented; we'd love to help unravel the supervenience relations between the human condition and brain properties.
