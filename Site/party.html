WHO: us


WHAT: contribute to the refinement of models relating our brains to our experiences


WHEN: now


WHY: we think that it is likely useful to operate under the following assumptions:


  1. •all of our mental properties (our thoughts, beliefs, preferences, ambitions, knowledge, etc.) are supervenient upon our brain properties (the stuff inside our heads)

  2. •our brains are well characterized* by networks (or graphs)

  3. •we can begin to model that relationship by using statistical decision theory applied to neuroscience and psychology


HOW:  party(!) in the following ways:


  1. •inferring brain-graphs from neural activity or anatomy (stay tuned)

  2. •inferring mental state using psychology (stay tuned)

  3. •using statistical decision theory to model the relationship between brain-graphs and mental properties (stay tuned)


we love this stuff, and there is plenty to do, so please do not hesitate to contact us if you want to join the party
